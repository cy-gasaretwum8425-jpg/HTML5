My Story

My name is Asare Twum Gideon, and I'm a student at the University of Mines and Technology (UMaT) studying BSc Cyber Security. Getting into this field wasn't just an academic decision for me — it came from a genuine curiosity about how the digital world works and, more importantly, how to keep it safe.

Growing up, I was always the person people came to when their phone was acting up or their laptop had a problem. I didn't always have the answers, but I loved trying to figure things out. That curiosity eventually led me to where I am today — learning about networks, security systems, digital threats, and how to defend against them.

I'm someone who takes my education seriously, but I also believe life is more than just books and exams. My faith, my family, and the friendships I've built along the way keep me grounded and motivated — especially on the tough days.

My Motto: "Stay humble, stay curious, and never stop growing."
